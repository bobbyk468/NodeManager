% Summary interpretation

ConceptGrade achieves statistically significant improvements on domain-specific CS datasets
(Mohler: $p = 0.0026$, 32.4\% MAE reduction; DigiKlausur: $p = 0.0489$, 4.9\% MAE reduction)
but shows no significant gain on the multi-domain Kaggle ASAG benchmark. This pattern suggests
that the knowledge graph approach is most effective for well-scoped CS domains where concept
relationships are predictable and the expertise to build domain KGs is available.
